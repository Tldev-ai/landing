Get Your Child’s Personalised IIT Success Roadmap

Imagine this. Your child has just stepped into Grade 12. Grade 11 is behind them — but deep down, you know something’s not right.

Those big coaching centres… Packed classrooms, fast-paced teaching, endless assignments. Even after revision sessions, your child still feels lost in many topics.

Now, there are only 6 months left for JEE Mains. Your child wants to give their 100% — but where should they start?

  • Which topics to focus on?
  • How to balance Grade 11 and Grade 12?
  • What’s the smartest way to use these 6 months?

That’s where we step in.

After our free 1-on-1 assessment session, our IIT/NIT mentor prepares a personalised roadmap just for your child:

  • We pick high-scoring topics from Grade 11 that your child can master quickly.
  • We focus on areas where the concepts are clear but numericals need practice.
  • We skip low-ROI confusion topics to save precious time.
  • We align Grade 12 chapters with the school’s pace, so your child doesn’t fall behind.
  • And most importantly — we design a plan that your child can actually follow without getting overwhelmed.

Finally, we sit down with you and your child to explain:

  • Where they stand right now
  • What’s achievable in 6 months
  • Exactly what to focus on for maximum JEE marks
Not just a study plan — a success map.

It’s a success map built around your child’s strengths, weaknesses, and time left — because every child is different.

Founded by Prudhvi — NIT Warangal

(Former Physics HOD Hyderabad)

I worked as a Physics HOD in a large coaching centre. Every day I watched a class of 80 race through the syllabus; 7–8 kept pace, many more slipped behind. Not because they were weak— but because the batch was too big and the curriculum too fast. Even when teachers wanted to help, there wasn’t time.

In 2017, I decided to build the opposite of that system.

  • 🎓100% 1-on-1 classes with IIT/NIT mentors
  • 🎯Only 50 students/year = quality over quantity
  • 📞Weekly parent reviews • instant strategy corrections

At iiTuitions, we teach one child at a time. We keep the intake to only 50 students a year so every child gets a plan, a mentor, and attention that’s impossible in big batches. We focus on interest first, learning next, and ranks as the outcome. With this approach, we’ve helped hundreds of students crack the JEE—without burning them out.

What I stand for

  • Quality over quantity — always
  • 1-on-1 mentorship by sharp, caring IIT/NIT faculty
  • Weekly parent reviews and course-corrections now, not after results
  • If a mentor’s style doesn’t fit your child, we switch—no ego
